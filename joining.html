Joining the Lab

Our interests range from "wet lab" molecular biology and embryology work with chick embryos, to genome-scale analyses within and across organisms, and even on to software development methodologies and open source software development. We are interested in working with computer scientists, biologists, physicists, engineers, and more.

The lab is opening its doors in summer of 2008, and we will be located on the third floor of Giltner Hall.

If you are already an MSU student and would like to visit the lab, please contact us directly.

If you are interested in applying to MSU to work with Dr. Brown, please contact him via e-mail with your C.V. and a (short!) description of what you're interested in doing.
